McGill Students Chinese Music Society

McGill Students Chinese Music Society (MSCMS) was formed in 2013 by McGill students with a vivid passion for traditional Chinese music culture with the goal of promoting various styles of traditional Chinese music and the exchange of occidental and oriental music within the McGill community.
